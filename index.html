About Me

With a strong background in core network operations and data infrastructure, Nasif brings several years of industry experience in managing large-scale telecommunication systems and roaming network services across South Asia and Southeast Asia. Before beginning his Ph.D., he held technical and strategic roles at Novotel Limited, Robi Axiata Limited, and Axiata Group Wholesale, where he contributed to system architecture design, roaming service integration, and network optimization for voice and messaging infrastructure.

Currently at Temple University, Nasif is involved in teaching and cutting-edge research projects such as intelligent task offloading in edge networks and the application of large language models in healthcare systems. His current focus lies in advancing scalable and intelligent cloud-networking solutions that enhance performance across distributed systems.

In addition to his research, Nasif holds several certifications in system administration, database management, and Python programming. He is fluent in English and Bangla and actively contributes to collaborative projects in academic and professional environments. His work is grounded in a deep understanding of both practical telecom challenges and theoretical advances in networked computing.
